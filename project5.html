This is a small website where I have used payment integration system. It's based on donation page where I have integrated Razor's payment. On the main page users can read about the donation and click the donate button.

After redirecting to the donation page, Razor's payment button will be available. Users can click the donate button and choose payment method to donate. After successfull payment the user will receive an invoice receipt.

This is a small website but the concept used here is integrating various techniques in the website. I have used Bootstrap to deisgn the website.
