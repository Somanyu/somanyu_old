This website is basically about implementing web designs or UI designs to the webpage. With few eye catching UI/UX ideas. I used a lot of CSS to handle all the designs in the webpage.

I also love to design websites rather than only developing. I focused on how to make a simple, static yet eye-catching website to attract the customers. I can't say it's the best but I learnt some few concepts and myths about web deisgning. I went through Dribble, a website for UI/UX designs.

I saw few designs in Dribble and tried to design my own in Adobe XD. Changed a few items and then implemented in the SCSS. I watched video about Dev Ed on Youtube, he explained very well about SCSS tips and tricks. The travel webpage was mainly focused on SCSS design, I am still learning about other CSS properties like shadows, transitions, etc.

Not to forget SCSS was used to design. I used Live SCSS Compiler to map the SCSS to CSS designs. SCSS was quite easy than CSS. There are still more ideas to come in the future which you will see on this website. Hope you liked it :)
